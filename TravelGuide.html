HOW TO GO RAGAY?
From Naga City, Ragay is located 67 kilometers to the north. It takes a maximum of two hours to reach Ragay via ground transportation. It will travel from Naga City to Sipocot, Camarines Sur via the Maharlika Highway and along Andaya Highway from Barangay Tara, Sipocot, Camarines Sur.
